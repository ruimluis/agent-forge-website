Concerns

We Hear You

Won't AI make my coaching feel impersonal?
AI handles the logistics your clients don't pay you for — scheduling, reminders, resource delivery. YOU still deliver the transformational coaching they hired you for. In fact, clients feel MORE supported because they get timely responses and resources 24/7.
I'm not tech-savvy enough for this
You don't need to be. We build it, we train it on your methodology, and we maintain it. You just tell us what you want it to do, like you would a human assistant — except it actually remembers everything perfectly.
This sounds expensive
A single new client pays for an entire year of AI automation. Most coaches recoup their investment in 30-45 days through time saved to close additional clients.
What if it makes mistakes with my clients?
We program guardrails so your AI partner knows when to escalate to you. Think of it like a well-trained assistant who says "Let me have [Coach Name] personally handle that" for anything nuanced.
