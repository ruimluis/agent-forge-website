FAQ

Common Questions

Won't AI make my service feel impersonal?
AI handles the logistics your clients don't pay you for — scheduling, reminders, resource delivery. YOU still deliver the transformational work they hired you for. In fact, clients feel MORE supported because they get timely responses and resources 24/7.
I'm not tech-savvy enough for this
You don't need to be. We build it, we train it on your methodology, and we maintain it. You just tell us what you want it to do, like you would a human assistant — except it actually remembers everything perfectly.
This sounds expensive
A single new client or placement pays for an entire year of AI automation. Most clients recoup their investment in 30-45 days through time saved to generate additional revenue. Compare our cost to a full-time hire at $40-60K plus benefits — it's a fraction of that.
What if the AI makes mistakes?
We program guardrails so your AI agent knows when to escalate to you. Think of it like a well-trained assistant who says "Let me have the team personally handle that" for anything nuanced. It handles routine tasks autonomously and flags anything unusual for human review.
How long does implementation take?
Most agents are fully operational in 2-3 weeks. We start with your highest-pain process to prove ROI fast. You provide your criteria, templates, and workflows — we handle all the technical setup.
Will this eliminate jobs on my team?
It eliminates tasks, not people. Your team members move UP to more strategic work, or you serve 2-3x more clients with the same headcount. Most businesses use AI to scale revenue, not cut staff.
