Concerns

Common Questions from Recruiting Leaders

Won't candidates hate talking to a bot?
They're not "talking to a bot" — they're getting instant responses to scheduling questions, quick answers about role details, and timely reminders. The AI handles logistics; your recruiters handle the actual conversations. Candidates actually prefer instant responses over waiting 2 days for a human to reply.
Our ATS already does some of this
Your ATS stores information. Our AI actively works your pipeline — proactively sourcing candidates, re-engaging cold leads, following up on no-responses, and identifying who's ready to move forward NOW. It's the difference between a filing cabinet and an assistant who never stops working.
What about candidate quality?
The AI learns YOUR definition of quality by analyzing your successful placements. It gets smarter over time and flags candidates who match your best performers. Plus, it pre-screens based on criteria you set, so you only interview candidates who actually meet requirements.
This sounds complicated to implement
We integrate with your existing ATS and tools. Most agencies are fully operational in 2-3 weeks. You give us your candidate criteria and communication templates — we handle the technical setup. Your team just starts getting better-qualified candidates, faster.
What if it makes mistakes?
AI agents follow rule-based logic you approve. For anything nuanced or sensitive, they escalate to human recruiters. Think of it like a junior recruiter who knows when to ask for help — except it never forgets, never gets tired, and handles 100 conversations simultaneously.
